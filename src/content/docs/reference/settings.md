The **Settings** page (`/settings`) is where you manage:

- **Profile** — your name and login details.
- **Team** — team name and members.
- **Billing** — your Kinderly subscription, payment methods, and add-ons. See [Billing](/billing/overview/).

## Manage settings

Kinderly Manage has its own **Manage → Settings** area with configuration specific to the CCMS module (meals, ratios, custom fields, Stripe Connect, integrations, CSV import). See [Manage → Settings](/manage/settings/).
