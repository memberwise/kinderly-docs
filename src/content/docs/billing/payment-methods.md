## The Stripe Customer Portal

Kinderly uses Stripe's **Customer Portal** for managing the financial side of your subscription. From **Settings → Billing** click **Manage Billing** to open the portal, where you can:

- Add, remove, or change the default payment method.
- Download past invoices.
- Update billing email and address.
- Cancel your subscription.
