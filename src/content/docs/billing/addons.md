Add-ons unlock extra features and are billed **per active child, per month**.

| Add-on | Name | Price | Requires |
|---|---|---|---|
| `manage` | **Kinderly Manage** | $0.85 / child / month | — |
| `manage_billing` | **Kinderly Manage — Billing** | +$0.20 / child / month | `manage` |

## Turning an add-on on or off

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Under **Add-ons**, toggle the add-on you want.
3. Confirm the change.

## Rules

- You must be on the **Pro** plan before you can enable any add-on.
- Turning off an add-on also turns off any add-ons that depend on it (e.g., turning off **Manage** also turns off **Manage — Billing**).
