> _Scaffolded — fill in the step-by-step walkthrough._

1. Go to **Packets** and click **New Packet**.
2. Drag a **form** or **document** from the palette onto the canvas.
3. Connect steps in the order you want parents to complete them.
4. Add an **action** where you want something automated (email, Grow card).
5. Add a **condition** where the flow should branch on an answer.
6. Save. Your packet is now ready to [share](/shares/overview/).
