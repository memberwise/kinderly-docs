Each packet has an **Action Trigger** setting that controls when automated [actions](/actions/overview/) run.

| Mode | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Immediate** | Actions fire the moment a parent finishes the step connected to them. |
| **Requires Approval** | No actions fire automatically. A staff member reviews the submissions and clicks **Approve** on the share detail page to run all actions at once. |

Use **Requires Approval** when you need a human to look things over — for example, before creating a Grow card or sending a welcome email.

## Approving a packet

When a family has completed every step of a packet in Requires Approval mode, the [share detail page](/shares/admin/) shows a **Needs Approval** badge with an **Approve** button. Clicking it runs all packet actions.
