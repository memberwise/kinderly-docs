You can attach [shares](/shares/overview/) directly to a Grow card, so the paperwork you've sent a family is always one click away from their pipeline entry.

## Add a share to a card

1. Open the card's detail dialog.
2. Scroll to the **Card Shares** section.
3. Click **Add Share to Card**.
4. Choose one of:
   - **Create new share** — fill out a new share; it's created and attached in one step.
   - **Link existing share** — search your team's existing shares and attach one.

## Removing a share

Click **Detach** on the attached share. The share itself is _not_ deleted — only its link to the card.

## Jumping to the share admin

Click **View share admin** on an attached share to jump to the [Share detail page](/shares/admin/).
