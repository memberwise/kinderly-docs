**Kinderly Grow** is a Kanban-style pipeline for tracking every family that reaches out to your center.

On your first visit, we auto-create a **Pipeline** board with five columns:

1. **Lead**
2. **Contacted**
3. **Tour Scheduled**
4. **Follow Up**
5. **Enrolled**

You can rename, reorder, add, or delete columns to match how your center works.
