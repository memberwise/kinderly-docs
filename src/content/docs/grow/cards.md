Each family in your pipeline is a **card**.

## Cards

- Cards have a **title**, a **description**, and color-coded **tags**.
- Drag a card up or down within a column to reorder it.
- Drag a card across columns to move the family to the next stage.
- Click a card to open its **detail dialog** — where you can edit details, add tasks, and attach shares.

## Tasks

Each card has its own **task checklist** — individual to-do items like _"Send welcome email"_ or _"Schedule tour"_.

- Check a task off when you complete it.
- Delete a task you no longer need.
