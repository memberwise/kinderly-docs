The Documents area is your center's PDF library. Upload files, group them into folders, and pull them into a [packet](/packets/overview/) when you send paperwork to a family.

## What you can do

- Upload PDFs and sort them into folders.
- Preview any document in the browser.
- Annotate and sign documents in place.
- Send a document to a parent as part of a packet.
