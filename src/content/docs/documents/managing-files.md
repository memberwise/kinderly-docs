> _Scaffolded — fill in the step-by-step._

1. Go to **Documents**.
2. Click **Upload** to add a new PDF.
3. Click **New Folder** to group related documents (e.g., "Enrollment 2026", "Policies").
4. Breadcrumbs at the top let you navigate back up the folder tree.
5. Use the search box to find a document by name.
