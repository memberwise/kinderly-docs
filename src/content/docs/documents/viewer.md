When you (or a parent) open a document in Kinderly, it loads in a built-in viewer — no download needed.

## What the viewer can do

- Flip through pages and zoom in or out.
- Fill in PDF form fields directly.
- Draw a signature with a finger or mouse.
- Undo a signature stroke at a time (or press **Ctrl + Z**).
- Save changes back to the original document.

## On phones and tablets

- The viewer opens at **75% zoom** on small screens so the page fits without sideways scrolling.
- You can pan around a zoomed-in page with your finger.
- Signing works with touch.
