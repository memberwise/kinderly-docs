The **Children** area (`/manage/children`) is where you manage every child's record across their time at your center.

## What you can track

Core info: name, birth date, gender, enrollment status, program, classroom, USDA eligibility.

Each child's detail page has tabs for:

- **Info** — core demographics and enrollment details
- **Family** — linked guardians with relationship type and custody flags
- **Medical** — conditions, physician info, medication
- **Allergies** — with severity
- **Immunizations** — with dates
- **USDA** — CACFP eligibility and income data
- **Billing** — linked billing accounts and fee overrides per program
- **Documents** — uploaded files
- **Attendance** — per-child sign-in/out log (individual or bulk entry)
- **Notes** — internal staff notes
- **Addresses** — multiple addresses per child
- **Custom Fields** — extra fields your center has defined

## New Child Wizard

A multi-step dialog that creates the child record and can create or link guardians in the same flow.

## Mass actions

Select multiple children to bulk-archive, bulk-assign to a program, and more.
