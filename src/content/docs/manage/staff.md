The **Staff** area (`/manage/staff`) holds a complete record for every employee.

## What you can track

Name, title, hire date, employment status, certifications, custom fields, addresses, notes, and app access.

Detail tabs:

- **Info** — core details and employment status
- **Certifications** — with expiration dates
- **Addresses**
- **Attendance** — sign-in/out history
- **Notes**
- **Custom Fields**
- **App Access** — the staff member's mobile app login
- **Documents**

## App Access

Give a staff member a login for the **Teacher mobile app**. From the App Access tab you can:

- Send them an invite.
- Reset their password.
- Block or unblock their access.

## Mass actions

Bulk operations across multiple staff members.
