**Classrooms** (`/manage/classrooms`) group children and staff together for scheduling and ratio tracking.

## What you can do

- Create, rename, archive, and restore classrooms.
- Assign children and staff to a classroom.
- Configure **ratio bands** — time-based child-to-staff ratios (e.g., stricter ratios for nap time, different during outdoor play).

## Ratio dashboard

The ratio dashboard computes per-band compliance in **real time** so you know at a glance whether you have enough staff on the floor in each room.
