The **Reports** area (`/manage/reports`) gives you flexible reporting across Manage data.

## Saved reports

A library of pre-built and saved custom report definitions. Open one, run it, and view or export the results.

## Custom report builder

Build a report from scratch using filters and columns across:

- Children
- Staff
- Attendance
- Billing
- …and other entities

Reports can be **exported** or **rendered inline**.
