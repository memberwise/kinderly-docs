**Programs** (`/manage/programs`) define the enrollment tracks families can choose from. Each program has:

- A **name**
- A **schedule**
- A **capacity**
- **Billing configuration** — one or more recurring **fees**

## Enrollment

Enroll a child into a program with a **start date** and optional **end date**. Each child's enrollment timeline shows active, future, and past enrollments.

## Fee overrides

Per-child overrides let you adjust a program's fee for individual children — for example, to apply a subsidy reduction or a sibling discount.
