**Manage Settings** (`/manage/settings`) is where you configure the Manage module for your whole center.

## Sections

- **Agency Info** — organization name, address, license number.
- **Meals** — configure meal types (breakfast, AM snack, lunch, PM snack, supper) and CACFP reimbursement rates.
- **Allergies Catalog** — the allergy types your staff can pick from on child records.
- **Immunizations Catalog** — the immunization types your staff can record.
- **Ratio Bands** — default ratio band templates applied to new classrooms.
- **Agency Ledgers** — funding source types (e.g., state subsidy programs).
- **Custom Fields** — extra fields your center wants on child, staff, or guardian records (text, date, select, checkbox).
- **Stripe Connect** — connect your Stripe account to accept card payments from guardians.
- **Integrations** — configure and enable third-party integrations like [KinderConnect](/integrations/kinderconnect/).
- **Import** — bulk import children or staff from a CSV.
