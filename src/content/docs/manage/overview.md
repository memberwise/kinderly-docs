**Kinderly Manage** turns Kinderly into a full childcare management system (CCMS). It's available as a metered [add-on](/billing/addons/) at **$0.85 per active child per month**.

With Manage you can run:

- Child, staff, and guardian records
- Classrooms and ratio tracking
- Programs and enrollment
- Attendance (desktop and mobile)
- Family billing and payments (separate add-on)
- Reporting
- Scheduling and time off
- Messaging with families
- State-agency integrations like **KinderConnect**

## Navigation

Manage has its own left-hand navigation under `/manage`:

**Dashboard · Children · Staff · Guardians · Classrooms · Programs · Billing · Reports · KinderConnect · Settings**

## Setup wizard

First-time providers are walked through setup at `/manage/setup` — agency details, meal types, ratios, classrooms, and programs.
