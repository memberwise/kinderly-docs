When a parent clicks the link you sent them:

1. They are asked for the **6-digit PIN**. After 5 wrong attempts the link is locked for 30 minutes.
2. Once the PIN is accepted, the paperwork opens:
   - **Form share** — the parent fills in the form and submits.
   - **Document share** — the parent views and signs the PDF.
   - **Packet share** — the parent moves through each step in order.

## Packet progress bar

On a packet share, a progress bar at the top shows every step:

- **Solid filled circle** — completed.
- **Outlined circle** — the step the parent is on right now.
- **Faint circle** — upcoming step.
- **Dashed circle** — a step that may or may not appear, depending on the parent's answers (conditional).
- **Greyed-out dashed circle** — a step that has been skipped because a condition took the other branch.

The parent never sees your branching logic — just the next thing they need to do.
