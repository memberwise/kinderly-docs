> _Scaffolded — fill in the step-by-step._

1. From a form, document, or packet, click **Send**.
2. Choose what to share, and enter:
   - **Title** — what the parent sees as the paperwork name.
   - **Recipient email** — the family member you are sending to.
   - Optional **expiration date** (after this, the link stops working).
   - Optional **maximum number of uses**.
3. Click **Create**.
4. Kinderly generates a **link** and a **6-digit PIN**. The PIN is shown **once** — deliver it to the parent separately (email, text message, etc.).

## Why a PIN?

The link on its own is not enough to open the paperwork. The parent must also type the PIN you sent them. After 5 wrong attempts the link is locked for 30 minutes.
