Actions are automation blocks you drop onto a [packet](/packets/overview/). When a family finishes the step an action is connected to, the action runs.

## Available actions

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Send email** | Sends an email — to a parent, a staff member, or anyone. |
| **Add card to Kinderly Grow** | Creates a new card in your [Grow](/grow/overview/) pipeline, so the family appears in the right column with optional tasks already attached. |

> More action types (webhooks, Slack, third-party integrations) are coming — see [Vendor integrations](/actions/vendor-integrations/).
