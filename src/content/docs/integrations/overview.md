Kinderly integrates with childcare-specific systems so you don't have to enter attendance or child data twice.

- [**KinderConnect**](/integrations/kinderconnect/) — report attendance to KinderSystems for state subsidy billing.
- [**Vendor integrations**](/actions/vendor-integrations/) — SIS and CRM systems (Salesforce, Infinite Campus, ProCare, Tyler SIS). Credential storage is available today; live actions are coming.
