Kinderly helps childcare programs run enrollment, paperwork, and day-to-day operations in one place. You can:

- Build the forms and packets families fill out during onboarding.
- Send paperwork to parents with a secure, PIN-protected link.
- Track prospective families through a visual pipeline (**Kinderly Grow**).
- Manage children, staff, classrooms, attendance, and billing with **Kinderly Manage**.
- Give teachers and parents a mobile app for daily check-in, messaging, and payments.
