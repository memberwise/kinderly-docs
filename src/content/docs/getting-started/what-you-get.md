Kinderly is organized into a few main areas. Use this page as a map — each link goes to a guide for that area.

| Area | What it's for |
|---|---|
| [Forms](/forms/overview/) | Build the forms families fill out. |
| [Documents](/documents/overview/) | Upload and organize PDFs — consent forms, handbooks, policies. |
| [Packets](/packets/overview/) | Combine forms and documents into a single onboarding flow. |
| [Shares](/shares/overview/) | Send paperwork to a specific family with a secure PIN link. |
| [Kinderly Grow](/grow/overview/) | A Kanban board for tracking families through your enrollment pipeline. |
| [Billing](/billing/overview/) | Your Kinderly subscription, seats, and add-ons. |
| [Kinderly Manage](/manage/overview/) | Run your center: children, staff, classrooms, attendance, family billing. |
| [Mobile Apps](/mobile/overview/) | Teacher and parent companion apps. |

> _Swap this table out for a diagram, a short video, or a "how it fits together" narrative for your program._
